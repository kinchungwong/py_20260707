# Session retrospective — 2026-07-09

**Theme:** stood up the streaming-audio foundation and the interactive-GUI spike
series (through input integration), and grew the `claude/` knowledge taxonomy to
match.

## What got built

Spikes (all with a `--selftest` headless mode; GUI ones also render a preview PNG):

- `outputstream_callback.py` — pull-model audio: `sounddevice.OutputStream` with a
  callback, mono, generating exactly the requested `frames` per call, 5 s run.
- `keyboard_static_display.py` — draw a two-octave keyboard, quit on Esc.
- `kbd_input.py` — QWERTY → MIDI, highlight on KEYDOWN/KEYUP, per-key dirty-rect redraw.
- `mouse_input.py` — mouse hit-test + glissando drag; black-key-first hit-testing.
- `input_integration.py` — `InputRouter` merges keyboard + mouse into one reconciled
  `NoteEvent` stream (reference-count by holder-set; one note-on / one note-off).
- `piano_keyboard.py` — **shared widget** (geometry, drawing, hit-test, `note_name`)
  extracted once the duplication became real; `kbd_input`/`mouse_input` refactored onto it.

## Decisions & new structure

- **Memory organized by first-layer domain** (subject area or library):
  `acoustics/`, `matplotlib/`, `musictheory/`, `pygame/`, `sounddevice/…`, `input/`.
  Principle written into `../memory/README.md` (path carries the topic; short leaf names).
- **Three new taxonomy folders**, each with a README:
  - `../policy/` — hard **off-limits** for safety/user-trust. First entry:
    `input-policy.md` bans `pygame.event.set_grab(True)` / any cursor confinement.
  - `../speculations/` — architectural ideas considered but **not committed**, with a
    revisit trigger. First entry: `audio-consumer-worker-thread.md` (topology B).
  - `../retrospectives/` — this folder.
- **Audio consumer = topology A (committed):** the audio callback drains the event
  queue directly. Topology B (worker thread + snapshot) parked as a speculation.
- **Input approach:** separate keyboard and mouse spikes, then integrate; pitch stays
  **12-TET / MIDI** for now, microtonal deferred (`microtonal_layout` spike).
- **Event vocabulary:** `NoteEvent(kind: NoteKind, midi, source: Source, t)` — enums
  for both `kind` and `source`; `perf_counter()` stamp captured at the source for the
  later queue/latency spikes.
